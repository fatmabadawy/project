Department of Neurology

            The Neurology Department at Holamed Hospital 
            provides outpatient clinic service in addition
            to hospital admission for emergency cases that
            require hospital treatment.The department is 
            distinguished by an elite group of the best 
            neurosurgeons in Egypt.

Department of Cardiology

            Holamed Hospital provides comprehensive 
            health care for cardiovascular patients 
            at the hands of elite Alexandria doctors 
            in heart diseases and surgery.

Department of Pediatrics and Neonatology

            The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit at Holamed 
            Hospital provides critical and non-critical 
            care for newborns suffering from diseases 
            and medical problems.

Department of Nephrology

            The Department of Dialysis and Diseases at 
            Holamed Hospital provides diagnostic and 
            therapeutic care to inpatients and 
            outpatients suffering from kidney-related 
            health problems.
