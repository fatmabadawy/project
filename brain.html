Department of Neurology

what we provide!

Neurosurgeons at Holamed Hospital have long experience in various types of surgical operations. Our goal is to ensure the correct initial diagnosis, as it is the first step to recovery, and the staff of the Neurology Department at Holamed Hospital is distinguished by working as a multidisciplinary team, relying on the latest advanced medical technologies and innovations to ensure patients’ comfort and obtain the best results.

  • Performing surgeries to remove brain tumors
  • Treatment of vascular diseases
  • Treatment of movement disorders
  • Epilepsy treatment
  • Treating spinal problems
  • Treating chronic pain
  • Treatment of traumatic brain injury
